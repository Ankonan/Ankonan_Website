ANKONAN and KNOWTECH have announced an agreement to share training services and the complete training technology portfolio, assisting the company in its efforts to bring its blockchain technology to his customers.


Location

KNOWTECH is located in the offices of Glories mall, on Avenida Diagonal, 188-198, right in the heart of the technological district of Barcelona 22 @. Being located in a shopping center we have parking for the students, as well as different means of transport, Glories stop of Line 1 of the subway, station Clot of Rodalies Renfe service or different bus lines and TramBesos that have their stop just in front of the building.





Classrooms and facilities

The offices are located on the fifth floor of the building, this has 2 lifts and both the entrance of the building and the offices are adapted for the disabled. All classrooms are exterior and have natural light, in terms of technical facilities, the classrooms are equipped with whiteboards, interactive projectors, Apple and Lenovo computers with the last processors and minimum of 16GB RAM memory, 23" monitors, Fibre channel Internet connectivity...
